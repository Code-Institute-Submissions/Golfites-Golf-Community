About Golfites

Our goal at Golfites is to provide Irish Golfers with an online community where they can connect with fellow golfers. Think of us as golfing social community similar to Facebook.

The Golfites idea came when we realised that there wasn't an online resource for golfers and specifically Irish golfers to come together in a golf specific group to connect and learn from each other. As golfers ourselves we realise that golf can be a very individualistic sport and that connecting with and learning from other golfers is an important aspect in improving our golf games.

Golfites members can avail of a number of our built-in resources which are aimed at providing the golfer with a unique online experience. We are like a one stop shop for all things golf related.

Below are the features available to Golfites Members:

  • Post Ads selling their used golf equipment and view others Ads.
  • Search through our 'golf course finder' which maps and details every one of the over 400 Courses in Ireland.
  • Purchase Equipment from the Golfites Shop.
  • Access our Golf Academy to learn golfing tips and hints from Golfites experienced contributors.
  • Read and post on the Golfites forums to discuss anything golf related.
  • Create their own Golfites profile with info about them and their golf, including video of their swing which can be commented on by other members.
  • Each Member can access their Member Dashboard for quick and easy access to each of the above features.
