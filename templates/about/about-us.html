About Us

Golfites was born from a strong passion for golf. We want to be a useful tool for those who love the game and want to connect with like minded people who share that passion and commitment.

Golfites' provides Irish Golfers with an online community where they can connect with fellow golfers. Think of us as golfing social community similar to Facebook. As golfers ourselves we realise that golf can be a very individualistic sport and that connecting with and learning from other golfers is an important aspect in improving our games. Golfites members can avail of a number of our built-in resources.

Golfites Ads

Golfites members can create adverts which will allow them to sell their old or used golf equipment. Creating an ad is free for Golfites Members and there are no sale fees attached by Golfites.

Golfites Forum

The Golfites forum is the place where members can discuss anything golf related. Members can create threads or post comments and give their opinions on a wide range of golfing subjects.

Golfites Equipment

Browse and purchase the newest golf equipment on Golfites. We stock a wide range of equipment from brands such as Taylormade, Titleist, Ping and Odyssey.

Search Golf Courses

Golfites users can search for and discover over 400 Irish golf courses using our course finder. It is the only interactive map showing every golf course in the country.

Golfites Academy

The Golfites Academy is a great place for members to learn and improve their games. Members gain access to exclusive content from our dedicated team of contributors.

Golfer Profiles

Members can create their own Golfites profile which can be viewed and commented on by other Golfites Members. Think of it as a golfing version of Facebook!
